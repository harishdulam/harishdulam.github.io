This project is focused on the automation of SAP maintenance checks. During scheduled downtime of the SAP system, the team is required to conduct essential checks before and after the maintenance window.

This project is committed to automating the data cleansing process for Electronic Point of Sales (EPOS) data, utilizing an ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipeline.
